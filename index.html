Apocalypse

Endless waves on a 2× open map (four slides). You start with 1000 HP; armor HP bonuses are ×10 here. Wave 1 spawns 3 random mobs, then +1 each wave. Rewards: $300 + $200 per wave; wave 5+ gives magicoin.

Bosses

Army Techno Dog (500 HP): Bite, Super Laser (purple), and Rapid Gattling Shot. Arena: Military Training Base (large map + extra center obstacles). Rewards: $300 and 1~3 Boss Crates.

Titan Zombie (700 HP): giant summoner + charge smash quakes. Arena: Military Training Base. Rewards: 2~4 Boss Crates.

Galaxy Warrior (1000 HP): aggressive slashes + meteor volley. Arena: Military Training Base. Rewards: 1~3 Boss Crates.
